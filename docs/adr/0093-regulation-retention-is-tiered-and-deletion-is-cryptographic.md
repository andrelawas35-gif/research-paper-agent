# Regulation Retention Is Tiered and Deletion Is Cryptographic

Raw narrative is not durable by default, encrypted temporary session text is discarded at compaction or closure, compact Regulation Records persist until user deletion, Safety Events remain minimal, and derived patterns are regenerated from remaining sources. Per-record encryption keys enable irreversible field redaction or Regulation Deletion within append-only storage, with tombstones preserving non-sensitive audit structure; exclusion, hiding, forgetting identity links, and physical purge remain distinct operations. This adds envelope-key management but gives deletion meaningful semantics across append-only logs and encrypted backups without claiming that every historical byte can always be physically rewritten.
