# Historical Backfill Is Selected, Bounded, and Reversible

Historical personalization begins only through an Owner-selected backfill that exposes available sources, date ranges, conversation counts, and estimated processing cost. The Owner selects domains and batches; Regulation and intimate relationship content are excluded by default; recent high-signal study, project, and explicit-values conversations are the recommended starting scope. Import creates Memory Candidates rather than accepted records, reports proposed values, preferences, commitments, conflicts, and discarded low-confidence material, and pauses when the Memory Inbox reaches its capacity limit. Cancellation removes derived candidates and disposable indexes, source links avoid unnecessary raw duplication, and imported material cannot be used to diagnose the Owner or another person. Expansion beyond the initial 30–60 day scope follows demonstrated candidate accuracy and usefulness.
