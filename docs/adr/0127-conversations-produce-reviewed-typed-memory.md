# Conversations Produce Reviewed Typed Memory

Historical and future conversations enter durable personalization through staged candidate extraction, sensitivity and domain classification, provenance attachment, duplicate and conflict detection, required Owner review for sensitive or identity-shaping claims, approved typed records, searchable projections, and task-specific retrieval. Raw conversation is not memory. Explicit low-risk operational facts may be accepted automatically, while values, purpose, Cognitive Support preferences, relationship interpretations, and recurring emotional patterns require confirmation before becoming authoritative. Every candidate retains source and timestamp provenance; contradictions create review items rather than overwrites; declined candidates remain suppressed unless materially new evidence appears; sensitive Regulation content stays outside the general embedding index; and the Owner can inspect why a record was retrieved. This permits cumulative adaptation without allowing temporary moods or model inference to redefine the Owner.
