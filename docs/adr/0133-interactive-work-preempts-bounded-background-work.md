# Interactive Work Preempts Bounded Background Work

The single production VM assigns highest priority to interactive web and API work and active Regulation Sessions, reserves bounded capacity for Discord delivery, scheduled-job claims, health checks, and backups, and treats embeddings, Historical Backfill, pattern review, OCR, and reindexing as preemptible background work. Background jobs obey CPU, memory, queue, and time-window limits, pause during interactive demand or elevated load, and resume from small durable batches. Each systemd service has CPU and memory accounting, limits appropriate to its role, health and restart policy; SQLite maintenance and backup operations remain short and observable. At 1 OCPU and 6 GB only one CPU-heavy background job may run, and no local LLM shares the host. A future 4-OCPU, 24-GB shape increases headroom without removing these controls.
