# First Daily-Use Scope Is Private PWA Regulation

The first daily-use approval covers the private PWA Regulation foundation only: guided check-ins, deterministic and offline regulation, the consented Orientation Snapshot, privacy governance, recovery, and model degradation. Discord rapid entry, scheduled reminders, longitudinal review jobs, and the general background dispatcher remain disabled until their channel-linking, delivery, consent, and separate-service acceptance suites are complete. This narrows the first launch scope in ADRs 0095, 0116, 0119, and 0120; it does not reverse their eventual architecture. No Discord or reminder process may be represented as production-ready or enabled by the installer in this release. The scope can expand only through a later ADR with end-to-end evidence for those processes.
