# GPT-5 Escalation Follows Consequence, Not Distress

GPT-5 may be selected when the Owner explicitly requests deeper analysis, a high-impact decision contains unresolved value conflicts, a broad verified review requires difficult synthesis, consequential research contains conflicting evidence, GPT-5 mini materially fails outside emergency Regulation, or an evaluation-approved workflow requires it. Emotional intensity, anxiety, repeated questions, reassurance seeking, conversation length, excessive retrieval, and uncertain background classification never qualify by themselves. Interactive escalation exposes the route change and estimated cost before use; only separately approved automatic workflows may escalate without a per-run prompt, and each requires a spending ceiling. This prevents distress and context-management failures from becoming billing signals or sources of over-analysis.
