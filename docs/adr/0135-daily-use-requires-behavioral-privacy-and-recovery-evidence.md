# Daily Use Requires Behavioral, Privacy, and Recovery Evidence

The Regulation Foundation Slice is not daily-use ready until representative Regulation and safety scenarios pass, code retains exclusive persistence and state-transition authority, malformed or unavailable model responses degrade predictably, and sensitive Regulation content is absent from logs, general retrieval, embeddings, and unencrypted recovery artifacts. Capture, correction, export, cryptographic deletion, restart and duplicate handling, interrupted-write recovery, offline safety access, and clean-environment restore from encrypted off-VM backup must work end to end. The PWA must remain usable on the Owner's phone under weak mobile connectivity, followed by a seven-day shadow-use period during which advice is evaluated, recording remains confirmed, and existing coping and safety resources stay available. Passing deployment or unit tests alone cannot establish daily-use readiness.
