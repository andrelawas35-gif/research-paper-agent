# Model Spend Is Bounded and Attributed

The model gateway enforces a configurable monthly budget, warnings at 50, 75, and 90 percent, per-workflow token and dollar ceilings, and a separate lower allowance for automatic background work. Automatic GPT-5 escalation requires workflow-specific permission; retrieval and response limits follow the task rather than the provider's context capacity; stable instructions are cache-friendly; and usage is attributed by mode, workflow, model route, and interactive or background origin. The initial warning budget is $20 per month, the hard limit is $30, and background work is limited to $5, subject to adjustment after one month of measured use. At the hard limit, background inference stops while an explicit Owner override can retain interactive GPT-5 mini access; the Deterministic Regulation Protocol remains available regardless of spend.
